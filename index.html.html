About Us & FAQ

Sip & Swirl On The Go was created with one mission in mind: to bring fun, unique, and unforgettable dessert and beverage experiences to every type of event — from weddings and birthdays to corporate gatherings and backyard celebrations.

We believe great events deserve great treats. That’s why we offer premium soft serve, froyo, Dole Whip, slushies, margaritas, frozen cocktails, milkshakes, and more — all served through commercial-grade rental machines that we personally deliver, set up, and pick up for you. No hassle, no stress, just pure enjoyment.

Whether you’re looking to elevate a wedding reception, add excitement to a birthday party, or surprise guests at a company event, our machines are the perfect way to create a memorable experience. With simple operation and high-capacity output, you can serve dozens — or even hundreds — of guests with ease.

FAQ

Do you deliver? Yes! We handle delivery, setup, and pickup for every rental.

Is product included? Absolutely — mixes and flavors are included with every package.

How far in advance should I book? We recommend booking as early as possible, especially during peak seasons.

Are the machines easy to use? Yes! We give you a full walkthrough, and they run automatically throughout your event.
